Puttanesca

Description

Very cheap and quick pasta

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil

  • 1 yellow onion

  • 4 cloves garlic

  • 3 anchovy filets

  • 1 28 oz crushed tomatoes

  • 1/2 tsp dried basil

  • 1/4 tsp crusehd red pepper

  • 1/2 cup sliced kalamata olivess

  • 2 tbsp capers

  • 1 lb spaghetti

    • Steps

    1. Dice the onion and mince the garlic. Add the olive oil, onion, garlic, and anchovy filets to a large skillet. Heat until the onions are soft and the anchovies have dissolved

    2. Add crushed tomatoes, basil, crushed red pepper, sliced olives, and capers. Stir then simmer.

    3. Biol water, add spaghetti, let cook for 7 minutes

    4. Combine pasta and sauce
