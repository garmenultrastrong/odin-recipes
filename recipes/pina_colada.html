Pina Colada

Description

Have a lovely drink on hot summers day.

  • 1 oz white rum

  • 1 oz cream of coconut

  • 1 oz pineaplle juice

  • 1/4 cup pineapple chunks

  • 1 cup of ice

  • 1 oz golden rum

    • Steps

  • Blend white rum, coconut cream, pineapple juice nad chunks, and ice

  • Pour into glass and top with golden rum
