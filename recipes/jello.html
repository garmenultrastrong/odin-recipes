St. Patricks Days Jell-O Shots

Description

Great for St. Patrivks Day, or if you are just feeling lucky. Serving size of 8

  • 1/2 package of Jell-O

  • 1 cup boiling water

  • 1/2 cup Jameson

  • 1 package Knox

  • 1/2 cup Baileys

    • Steps

    1. PLaceshot glasses in muffin tin at an angle

    2. Pour lime gelatin mix into a bowl. Add 1/2 cup boiling water and stir until dissolved. Add Jameson and stir. Pour mixture carefully into each shot glass, filling to near the top of the slanted glasses. Refrigerate about 4 hours

    3. Pour unflavored gelatin into a bowl. Add 1/2 cup boiling water and stir. Let rest 3 to 5 minutes. Add Baileys and stir

    4. Remove glasses from tin and place upright. Top with strawberry mixture. Refrigerate and chill about 4 hours or more
